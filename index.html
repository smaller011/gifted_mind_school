About Us

For over 13 years, Gifted Mind International School has been a center of academic excellence. We offer Kindergarten, Nursery, Primary, and Secondary education, creating a seamless learning journey for children as they grow.

Our Vision

To nurture young minds with excellence, integrity, and creativity, preparing students to make a positive impact in their communities and the world.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that builds strong academic, moral, and social foundations for lifelong success, in a safe and supportive environment.
